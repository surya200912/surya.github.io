About the Project

P.E.A.R.L x L.U.M.E.N is an advanced dual‚Äëpurpose innovation built to tackle two major challenges: flood safety and black water reuse. It is designed with modular hardware so it can be deployed in rural villages, urban cities, or disaster-prone coastal regions.

The P.E.A.R.L (Predictive Emergency Alert & Rescue Lights) system uses water pressure, GPS, and climate sensors to detect early flood risks. It sends instant alerts to local authorities and residents via GSM technology, ensuring maximum evacuation time.

The L.U.M.E.N (Light Utilization for Muddy Environments Navigation) system uses high-intensity LED arrays, laser beacons, and color-coded paths to guide people even through muddy floodwaters. It also powers a solar-driven filtration unit that treats black water through multi-layer sand & charcoal filtration followed by UV sterilization.

System Working

  1. Detects abnormal water rise via sensors.
  2. Verifies environmental conditions with climate module.
  3. Sends SMS alerts to officials and residents.
  4. Activates LED/laser guidance system.
  5. Filters black water for non-potable uses.
